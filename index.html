About Our School

Building character and intellect for a better tomorrow.

Our Legacy

Established in 1994, J.R. International Hr. Sec. School has been a pioneer in providing quality education in Ghaziabad. We are affiliated with the State Board and work in association with J.R. Global Association.

We aim to provide a safe, nurturing environment where students are encouraged to explore their potential. Our motto "Good People, Good School" reflects our commitment to moral values.
