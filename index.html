About Us

Our Legacy

Established in 1994, J.R. International Hr. Sec. School is a premier English Medium institution. We are Government Recognized and affiliated with the State Board. Our school operates in association with J.R. Global Association and J.R. International.


Mission & Vision

We aim to provide holistic education that balances academic rigor with moral values. Our vision is to shape students who are not only successful professionals but also compassionate human beings.

Principal's Message

"As Principal, I am committed to fostering an environment of excellence and compassion. Education is not just about books; it is about building character. We welcome you to join our family."


- Principal, J.R. International
